About Us

Bigtime Empire Corporation is the home of some of the most beloved and iconic brands in the Philippines. With a passion for delivering high-quality food and exceptional experiences, we proudly bring you the flavors of Pares Retiro, known for its savory pares and Filipino comfort food; The Sandwich Guy, a fresh and healthy alternative for sandwich lovers on the go; Cali Pizza, offering a taste of California with bold flavors and the freshest ingredients; California Beach Pansol, a go-to destination for relaxation and recreation by the beach; Sapporo Milk Tea House, serving a variety of delicious and unique milk tea blends; and Kalye Express, providing fast, flavorful street food-inspired meals.

At Bigtime Empire Corporation, we believe in bringing people together through exceptional food, unforgettable experiences, and a commitment to quality. Our brands have become household names, serving communities with the best flavors and services. Here at Bigtime, we do things differently.
